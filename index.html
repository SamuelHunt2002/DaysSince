My pledges for the Party.

If elected, I will not sit around, but bring big changes quick.

Creating transparrency within the Exec.

I will make sure that our exec becomes more transparrent, with fortnightly Q and A's similiar to MQ's.

A buddy system.

I will introduce a Buddy System to the Party, so newer people can be more welcomed.

Better relations for other parties.

I reguarly talk to members of the Government, OO and UO. Because of this, I can improve our relations with other parties.

Reforming Conference.

With me as PP, I will make conference more inclusive, as well as fun. This will be through speeches, awards and other activities made to engage everyone.

Fortnightly polls.

On the weeks that aren't having a Q and A, I will introduce polls ranging from views on our direction, political views and more trivial things.

Press Outlet.

I have had extensive experience in MBBC, and if elected I plan on introducing a Party Press Release similiar to the old Vanguard Press Release. This will make us look stronger to the outside world.

My Story.

Ever since being elected as a National MP, I have always fought for the Party.

When elected a National MP, in the early days of /u/Bznss's time as Leader, I didn't know what to expect. However as time went on I understood what it meant to be a Liberal Democrat. It means to always see the best in people and situations. To be committed to freedom and equality. With me as Party President, I will keep fighting for these values.
